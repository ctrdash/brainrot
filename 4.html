Tyler Blevins Has Quit Fortnite

A few days ago, popular fortnite streamer Tyler Blevins was playing a solo game. He landed

at Grim Gate. While he was fighting the Cerberus boss, who drops the mythic Gatekeeper Shotgun,

a player who goes by the name of DarkVoid2234 pushed Grim Gate using one of the season's many

vehicles. DarkVoid2234 abused one of Fortnite's new weapons, known as the Nitro Fists. Many people

online say the Nitro Fists need to be vaulted, and clearly Blevins agrees with this. DarkVoid2234

destroyed Blevins using the new Nitro Fists. From there, Blevins immediately closed the game and shut

his stream off. We attempted to contact both Blevins and DarkVoid2234, but neither of them have responded.

A YouTube channel that goes by the name of Daily Clips Central uploaded the clip of Blevins quitting

the game. You can watch the clip in the embedded video below. Credits to Daily Clips Central (Youtube) and

Tyler Blevins (Twitch).
