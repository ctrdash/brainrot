Another News Website Has Popped Up Online

A GitHub user has released his website called Brainrot News, a fresh, and un-serious news

website. The creator of the website says "This website shouldn't be taken seriously. I'm probably

going to forget to update the articles at some point." Brainrot News publishes 3 articles a month,

and the 3 previous articles are sent to the "Archives" page.
