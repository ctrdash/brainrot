Should Nintendo Reopen The "Miiverse" (DISCUSSION)

The following was a privately-held discussion between Mark Smith and Jake Smith.

Mark: Hey, welcome to the Brainrot Discussion. Recently, a relic from the Wii U era has gone viral.

Jake: What do you mean?

Mark: Elon Musk, the CEO of Twitter (2022-Present) recently made the decision to hide likes.

Jake: Well that's kinda dumb.

Mark: It is, so people rebelled by posting images of the "Yeah!" button that Miiverse used.

Jake: What's Miiverse?

Mark: Miiverse was a social media platform for Nintendo's Wii U and 3DS consoles. It got shut down

back in 2017. Nintendo claimed it was because of "people moving to other platforms" but I believe it

was because of the posts people made on the Miiverse.

Jake: What kind of posts were made?

Mark: Some posts were bad, others were just kind of dumb. We won't get into the posts in specific though.

Jake: Ok.

Should Nintendo bring back the Miiverse? Let us know!
