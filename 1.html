Clix Reveals He's Bringing Back Several OG Cosmetics In His New Locker Bundle

A pro fortnite player who goes by the name of "Clix" has revealed that on June 24th, 2024,

his locker bundle will be returning to the Fortnite Item Shop. Alongside this, he's revealed

that he has a new locker bundle releasing. He said that this locker bundle will have "1000+

day cosmetics." We now also have an idea of what we can expect from this locker bundle.

It was revealed that fortnite's OG NFL skins have recieved and update to recieve lego styles.

Ali-A recently revealed that the Fortnite item shop has a rule where if a cosmetic does not work

in LEGO Fortnite, said cosmetic cannot return to the item shop.
